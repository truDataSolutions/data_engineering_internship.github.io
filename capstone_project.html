Capstone Project

For this project, you will be synthesizing the skills and knowledge you've gained over the course of of this internship. The goal is to create an end-to-end pipeline. You will select a dataset of your choosing, but a good place to find one is HERE. Once you' chosen your dataset perform EDA on it. Try to determine what critical questions you'd like to answer. The next step will be to create a pipeline with the tool of your choosing. You can create an ETL or ELT pipeline. Lastly, create a presentation and visualizations to explain your findings to key personel. You will be presenting this to a large majority of truData.
