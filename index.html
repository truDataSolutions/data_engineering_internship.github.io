Introduction

Firstly, we would like to welcome you to the truData team. truData Solutions has been working with several partners to provide data, analytics, and integration services on several cloud platforms. The goal of this internship is to teach you the real-world skills, tools, and applications for deployment in a professional environment. This website is broken into multiple modules. We strongly recommend you go through each section. At the end of the internship, you will demo your capstone project to the team to demonstrate your understanding on the topics covered.

It is important to document your progress during this internship. To do so, we suggest documentation software such as Miro. This will allow you to store your notes and some exercises for reference later. The purpose of documentation is to demonstrate understanding and workflow of your projects.
