Introduction

Firstly, we would like to welcome you to the truData team. truData Solutions has been working with several partners to provide data, analytics, and integration services on cloud platforms. The goal of this internship is to teach you the real-world skills and applications for deployment in a professional environment. During your internship you will learn about:


Data Engineers

Their purpose and how they work

The Agile Methodology

What is Agile and what are the principal and values

Python

How it can be used, the basics, libraries, and data ingestion and manipulation

SQL

Basic and advanced SQL

Databricks

What it is and how Spark is used with it

Database Architecture

The difference between data warehouses, data lakes, and delta lakes

Database Models

Different database models such as Star schema, relational database, and one big table

Data Pipelines

What a data pipeline is, ETL, and ELT

Data Engineering Tools

The Leading providers for data engineering tools

Cloud Storage Offerings

The different storage offerings from AWS

This website is broken into multiple modules. We are assuming you do not have prior knowledge on many of the tools and applications introduced, thus you can skip material you feel you have a strong understanding on. However, we strongly recommend you go through each section. At the end of the internship, you will demo your capstone project to the team to demonstrate your understanding on the topics covered.

It is important to document your progress during this internship. To do so, we suggest documentation software such as Miro. This will allow you to store your notes and some exercises for reference later. The purpose of documentation is to demonstrate understanding and workflow of your projects.
